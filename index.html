About Me

My name is Jorge Alfonso and I am a Systems Engineer with experience in web development in Java as a back-end language and front-end languages such as JavaScript, HTML 5 and CSS with additional expertise in enterprise applications and additional knowledge in SOA and web developement frameworks like Richfaces and AngularJS.

I have a great passion in new and innovative technologies such as HTML5, CSS3 and especially those that deal with JavaScript Frameworks and underlying techs like Node.js, Mongodb, Bower, Gulp, Karma, Protractor, etc.

I have participated in the One Laptop per Child project and I have created and developed important projects here in Colombia in the past such as the implementation of the Mobile Portability Number for the Telefonica Movistar Provider, the web application Dutch auctions and Demands Firm for the Colombian Stock Exchange (BVC), the web portal for Infovalmer, the web aplications Judicial Deposits, Multicámara and PSE for the Bank Agrario of Colombia.

I have migrated the SOA arquitecture of the Caja Social Bank.
